About Me

You wouldn’t let a stranger walk into your home, right? I believe the same applies to a company. That’s why I’d like to tell you a bit about myself—and when the time comes, I’d love to hear about you too.

I’m Emilio Chavez, a marketing student at Tecnológico de Monterrey, with international experience at Ajou University in South Korea. I’m passionate about digital marketing, neuromarketing, and sales.

Sports and discipline have always been a big part of my life—I currently practice swimming, climbing, and going to the gym. Since I was a kid, I’ve loved drawing, though I never cared about being the best or following strict rules. Instead, I was drawn to the creative side of it. This led me to design clothing, but soon I faced a bigger challenge: how to sell my designs. I researched, experimented, and one day, while watching the news (I’ve always enjoyed watching news programs), I saw a report about a brand that had skyrocketed sales through an incredible marketing campaign. That moment sparked my fascination with marketing and the creative process behind selling.
